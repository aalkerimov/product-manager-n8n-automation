# Changelog — Persona Generator

## [0.1.0] — 2026-07-14

### Added
- Initial release (beta)
- Manual Trigger with ProjectFlow SaaS research sample (4 inputs, 3 segments)
- LLM: research findings → evidence-grounded personas with goals, frustrations, behavioural patterns, retention signals, product implications, and evidence citations
- Persona matrix narrative
- JSON validation with error routing
- PM approval gate with 48h timeout
- Rejection logging

### Known issues at release
- Not runtime-tested
- Persona quality is proportional to research input quality
- LLM personas are structured hypotheses, not empirically validated archetypes
- Retention signal field requires quantitative product analytics to be meaningful
